# 人类健康计划基金会 (HHPF)

## 项目概述
HHPF致力于推动从“生物医学“到“系统医学”与“智能健康”融合的跃迁，构建全球普惠的健康基础设施，深化新时代健康科技、弘扬新时代健康文化，推动新时代人类健康文明。

## 核心使命
- 打破健康信息壁垒，普及健康教育
- 利用AI技术整合各类疗法，构建统一的健康逻辑框架
- 推动自然疗法的量化验证与标准化
- 实现健康服务的普惠化，让优质健康指导惠及全球

## 项目背景
站在智能时代正式到来的历史节点，人类健康事业正经历着自抗生素发明以来最深刻的范式转移。我们不再仅仅处于"生物医学时代"，而是正式迈入了"智能系统健康时代"。

## 当前挑战
1. **过度医疗与医源性伤害**：全球范围内普遍存在过度检查与过度用药现象
2. **碎片化治疗与割裂的医养体系**：传统医学分科过细，医养长期割裂
3. **健康服务不平等**：优质医疗资源集中在少数地区和人群

## 解决方案：SMD范式
基于SMD（Systems Medicine Doctor，系统医学诊疗）的全新范式，核心在于：
- **回归健康的第一性原理**：健康被定义为能量（Energy）× 结构（Structure）× 流动（Flow）的动态平衡
- **医养一体化**：医学疗法与自然疗法的和谐统一
- **AI驱动的精准决策**：利用AI大数据实现全生命周期的最优健康决策

## 核心举措
1. **自然疗法疗效量化**：推动全球最大的自然疗法A/B实验工程
2. **SOP标准化推广**：制定全球通用的健康SOP手册
3. **公益与商业平衡**：公益为体，商业为用，实现可持续发展

## 愿景
让健康服务像水电煤一样，通过AI算法和SOP低成本地流向全球每一个角落，成为人类健康的基础设施。

# Human Health Project Foundation (HHPF)

## Project Overview
The Human Health Project Foundation (HHPF) is dedicated to promoting the transition from biomedicine to the era of intelligent system health, building a globally inclusive health infrastructure.

## Core Mission
- Break down health information barriers and popularize health education
- Integrate various therapies using AI technology to build a unified health logical framework
- Promote quantitative verification and standardization of natural therapies
- Achieve universal access to health services, making quality health guidance available worldwide

## Project Background
Standing at the historical node of 2025, human health is experiencing the most profound paradigm shift since the invention of antibiotics. We are no longer merely in the "biomedical era" but have officially entered the "intelligent system health era."

## Current Challenges
1. **Over-medicalization and iatrogenic harm**: Widespread over-examination and over-medication globally
2. **Fragmented treatment and disconnected medical-care system**: Traditional medicine is overly specialized, with long-term separation between medical treatment and care
3. **Health service inequality**: High-quality medical resources are concentrated in a few regions and populations

## Solution: SMD Paradigm
A new paradigm based on SMD (Systems Medicine Doctor), with core principles:
- **Return to the first principles of health**: Health is defined as the dynamic balance of Energy × Structure × Flow
- **Integration of medical treatment and care**: Harmony between medical therapies and natural therapies
- **AI-driven precise decision-making**: Utilizing AI big data to achieve optimal health decisions throughout the life cycle

## Core Initiatives
1. **Quantification of natural therapy efficacy**: Promoting the world's largest A/B experimental project for natural therapies
2. **SOP standardization promotion**: Developing globally universal health SOP manuals
3. **Balance between public welfare and business**: Public welfare as the foundation, business as the means, to achieve sustainable development

## Vision
To make health services flow to every corner of the world at low cost through AI algorithms and SOPs, just like water, electricity, and coal, becoming the infrastructure for human health.
